## Lexora AI — Brand Context

> **Learn Smarter. Speak Better.**

**Product:** AI-powered English learning platform with adaptive lessons, intelligent tutoring, speech evaluation, and real-time feedback.

**Markets:** Vietnam (primary) → Southeast Asia (expansion)

**Mission:** Empower millions of learners to communicate confidently in English through personalized AI-powered education.

**Vision:** Become the most trusted AI English learning platform in Vietnam and expand into a global language learning ecosystem.

### Core Values

| Value | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Personalized Learning | Unique paths based on goals, strengths, and weaknesses |
| Practical English | Real-life communication, workplace English, exam prep |
| AI with Purpose | Teach, explain, coach, motivate — not just provide answers |
| Continuous Improvement | Learning adapts dynamically to performance |
| Accessible Education | High-quality, affordable English education for everyone |

### Target Users

- **Students** — graduation requirements, TOEIC, scholarships, internships
- **Professionals** — career advancement, international workplaces, presentations
- **Job Seekers** — interviews, TOEIC requirements, communication skills
- **English Centers** — student management, AI-assisted teaching, progress tracking
- **Enterprises** — workforce training, analytics, business English programs

### Product Ecosystem

| Product | Status | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Lexora TOEIC | Current | Adaptive prep, mock exams, improvement plans |
| Lexora Speaking | Current | Pronunciation, fluency, grammar, confidence coaching |
| Lexora Writing | Current | Grammar correction, vocabulary, writing evaluation |
| Lexora Business | Current | Meetings, emails, presentations, negotiations |
| Lexora Interview | Current | Mock interviews, speaking assessments, feedback |

## Output Rules

When generating any Lexora AI artifact (PRD, spec, copy, prompts, UX text):

1. **Voice** — Match brand voice; learner-facing content must be simple and motivating.
2. **AI behavior** — Features must teach, explain, coach, and motivate — never just give answers.
3. **Market** — Design for Vietnamese learners first; consider Southeast Asia expansion.
4. **Practicality** — Prioritize real communication and exam outcomes over abstract grammar drills.
5. **Accessibility** — Assume mixed income levels; avoid premium-only assumptions in core flows.
6. **Quality** — Enterprise-grade structure: clear goals, users, requirements, acceptance criteria, metrics.
7. **Phasing** — Respect product roadmap; do not scope Future products as Current MVP.
