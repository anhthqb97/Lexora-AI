# Center Onboarding Playbook

**Task:** P2-T24  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-19

---

## Week 0 — Sign-up

1. Center director completes intake form
2. Lexora creates center record in admin portal
3. Import student roster (CSV: name, email, class)

---

## Week 1 — Launch

1. Teacher training (60 min): assignments + progress dashboard
2. Students receive login + first Speaking assignment
3. Weekly check-in with center contact

---

## Week 2–4 — Adoption

- Monitor completion rates in teacher dashboard
- Adjust assignment difficulty based on level distribution
- Collect center feedback via NPS

---

## Escalation

| Issue | Owner | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| Login failures | Support | 24h |
| Low completion | CS + PM | 48h |
| Payment / billing | Finance | 72h |
