# Center Pilot Recruitment Plan

**Task:** P2-T23  
**Target:** 10 English center pilots by M8  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-19

---

## Ideal Pilot Profile

- 50–300 active students
- Existing TOEIC or Speaking curriculum
- Willing to assign Lexora homework weekly
- Ho Chi Minh City or Hanoi preferred (Phase 5)

---

## Recruitment Channels

1. Direct outreach to center directors (LinkedIn, referrals)
2. Lexora beta alumni who train at centers
3. Education conferences (VUS, Apollo partner events)

---

## Pilot Offer

- 3 months free Pro for all enrolled students
- Dedicated onboarding session (see playbook)
- Co-branded progress reports for parents

---

## Success Criteria

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Pilots signed | 10 |
| Active weekly usage | ≥60% of assigned students |
| Center NPS | ≥40 |
