# Overview

Lexora AI is an AI-powered English learning platform that delivers personalized learning experiences through adaptive lessons, intelligent tutoring, speech evaluation, and real-time feedback.

Designed initially for Vietnamese learners, Lexora AI helps students, professionals, and organizations improve English communication skills while preparing for international exams such as TOEIC, IELTS, and TOEFL.

Our long-term vision is to become the leading AI education platform in Southeast Asia.

# Product Requirements

## Scope & Non-Goals

### In Scope (Phase 1 — Current)

* Individual learner accounts with personalized learning paths
* Lexora TOEIC, Speaking, Writing, Business, and Interview modules
* AI tutoring with explanations, not answer-only responses
* Speech evaluation (pronunciation, fluency, grammar, vocabulary)
* Writing correction with structured feedback
* Progress tracking and analytics for learners
* Web and mobile-responsive experience
* Vietnamese UI with English learning content
* Subscription-based pricing for individual learners

### Out of Scope (Phase 1)

* Additional target languages (Japanese, Korean, Chinese)
* Lexora Kids, Lexora for Schools, Lexora Enterprise
* Offline-first mode or native desktop apps
* Live human tutoring marketplace
* Content authoring tools for third-party creators
* HR system integrations (SAP, Workday, etc.)
* Proctored certification issuance (Lexora does not replace official TOEIC/IELTS exams)

### Non-Goals

* Lexora AI is not a general-purpose chatbot or homework-completion tool
* Lexora AI does not guarantee specific exam scores
* Lexora AI does not replace classroom teachers — it augments learning
* Lexora AI does not store or share learner data for advertising purposes

## Technical Constraints

### Platforms

* **Web:** Next.js 15 — modern browsers (Chrome, Safari, Edge); mobile-responsive (MVP primary client)
* **Mobile native:** Expo (React Native) — Phase 4, after beta retention ≥ 35%
* **Application (MVP):** Modular monolith — see [`docs/engineering/architecture-decision-record.md`](../../engineering/architecture-decision-record.md)
* **Application (target):** Microservices — extract ai-gateway, speech, billing first
* **Database:** MongoDB Atlas (Singapore region)
* **LLM (MVP):** OpenAI GPT-4o server-side; Ollama for local dev
* **LLM (target):** Self-hosted vLLM + LiteLLM when cost or PDPD requires
* **Infra (MVP):** Vercel + Atlas + Upstash Redis
* **Infra (target):** Docker + Kubernetes when MAL > 50K

### Performance

* Page load: ≤3s on 4G connection (Vietnam average)
* Speech recognition: real-time or near-real-time (<2s processing)
* Support concurrent sessions: 10,000+ simultaneous learners (Phase 1 target)

### AI & Speech

* LLM-powered tutoring with guardrails against answer-only behavior
* Speech-to-text and pronunciation scoring engine required
* Support Vietnamese-accent English evaluation (primary use case)
* AI responses must be age-appropriate and exam-relevant

### Localization

* UI language: Vietnamese (primary), English (secondary)
* Learning content: English with Vietnamese explanations where helpful
* Currency: VND for Vietnam; regional currencies for SEA expansion

### Integrations (Phase 1)

* Payment: MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay, credit/debit cards
* Authentication: Email, phone (OTP), Google, Facebook
* Analytics: Product analytics and error monitoring

## Compliance & Privacy

### Data Protection

* Comply with Vietnam's Personal Data Protection Decree (PDPD) and applicable SEA regulations
* Learner data stored in-region where required by law
* Explicit consent for voice recording and speech analysis
* Right to delete account and associated learning data
* No sale of personal data to third parties

### Minors (Lexora Kids — Future)

* Parental consent required for users under 16
* Age-gated content and restricted data collection
* COPPA-aligned practices for international expansion

### AI Safety

* Content moderation for inappropriate learner or AI-generated content
* No generation of exam answers without educational context
* Transparent AI disclosure — learners know they interact with AI
* Human escalation path for reported issues

### Accessibility

* WCAG 2.1 AA target for web interface
* Screen reader compatibility for core learning flows
* Adjustable text size and high-contrast mode (Phase 2)

## Competitive Positioning

### Market Context

The Vietnam English learning market includes exam prep apps, language learning platforms, English centers, and general AI chatbots. Lexora AI competes by combining exam-focused outcomes with AI tutoring designed for Vietnamese learners.
