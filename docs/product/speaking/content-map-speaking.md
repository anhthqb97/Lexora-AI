# Content Map — Lexora Speaking

**Feature:** Lexora Speaking
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-19

Content inventory for MVP and P1. AI openers and follow-ups generated from these seeds.

---

## 1. Topics (MVP — 10)

| ID | Topic | Level | Sample opener | Sample follow-ups |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| T-01 | Travel | A2–B2 | "Have you traveled anywhere interesting recently?" | Where did you go? What did you eat? Would you go back? |
| T-02 | Technology | B1–B2 | "What's one app you use every day?" | Why is it useful? How has technology changed your life? |
| T-03 | Health & Fitness | A2–B1 | "How do you stay healthy?" | Do you exercise? What's your favorite food? |
| T-04 | Education | A2–B2 | "Tell me about your school or university." | What's your favorite subject? Why do you study English? |
| T-05 | Food & Cooking | A2–B1 | "What's your favorite Vietnamese dish?" | Can you describe how to make it? Do you like foreign food? |
| T-06 | Hobbies | A2–B1 | "What do you like to do in your free time?" | How often do you do it? Why do you enjoy it? |
| T-07 | Work & Career | B1–B2 | "Tell me about your job or dream job." | What skills do you need? What's challenging about it? |
| T-08 | Family & Friends | A2–B1 | "Tell me about your family." | Who are you closest to? What do you do together? |
| T-09 | Environment | B1–B2 | "What environmental issues concern you?" | What can individuals do to help? |
| T-10 | Daily Routine | A1–A2 | "What time do you usually wake up?" | What do you do in the morning? Describe a typical day. |

## 2. Scenarios (MVP — 5)

| ID | Scenario | Level | AI role | Learner goal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| S-01 | Restaurant | A2–B1 | Waiter | Order food, ask about menu, pay bill |
| S-02 | Job Interview | B1–B2 | Interviewer | Introduce yourself, answer strengths/weaknesses |
| S-03 | Hotel Check-in | A2–B1 | Receptionist | Check in, ask about amenities, request help |
| S-04 | Doctor Visit | B1 | Doctor | Describe symptoms, understand advice |
| S-05 | Shopping | A2 | Shop assistant | Ask prices, sizes, return policy |

### S-02 Job Interview — question bank

1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why do you want this job?
3. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
4. Describe a challenge you faced at work.
5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
6. Do you have any questions for us?

## 3. TOEIC Speaking Prompts (P1)

| ID | Task type | Format | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| TS-01 | Read a text aloud | 45 sec prep, 45 sec speak | Short announcement paragraph |
| TS-02 | Describe a picture | 45 sec prep, 45 sec speak | Office meeting scene |
| TS-03 | Respond to questions | 3 sec prep, 15 sec each | "What kind of movies do you like?" |
| TS-04 | Respond using information | 45 sec prep, 60 sec speak | Schedule table → answer questions |
| TS-05 | Express an opinion | 45 sec prep, 60 sec speak | "Do you prefer working alone or in a team?" |

**MVP subset:** TS-02 (picture description) + TS-03 (respond to questions) only.

## 4. Free Talk Seeds

When learner selects Free Talk, AI uses goal + level from profile to pick opener:

| Goal | Opener style |
|---|---|
| TOEIC | Exam-related small talk, study habits |
| Speaking | Daily life, opinions, descriptions |
| Business | Workplace topics, meetings, emails |
| General | Friendly casual conversation |

## 5. Level Adaptation Guide

| CEFR | Vocabulary | Sentence length | Correction style |
|---|---|---|---|
| A1 | High-frequency words only | 5–8 words | Vietnamese help offered |
| A2 | Common daily vocabulary | 8–12 words | Simple corrections |
| B1 | Broader topics, some idioms | 12–18 words | Explain-why corrections |
| B2 | Abstract topics, formal register | 15–25 words | Nuanced feedback |
| C1 | Complex arguments | 20+ words | Style and precision focus |
