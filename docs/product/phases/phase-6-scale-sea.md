## Objectives

1. Launch Lexora Enterprise and Lexora for Schools (full LMS)
2. Expand to Southeast Asia (Thailand, Indonesia, or Philippines first)
3. Add Lexora Kids
4. Multi-language platform foundation (Japanese, Korean, Chinese — future)
5. Full microservices on Kubernetes
