# Beta Learner Recruitment Plan — Closed Beta (50 learners)

**Task:** P1-T061  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-19  
**Owner:** PM  
**Gate:** M3 — closed beta start

---

## 1. Objective

Recruit **50 Vietnamese English learners** for a 2-week closed beta of Lexora Speaking before TOEIC launch.

| Segment | Target | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| University students | 30 | Primary TOEIC audience; graduation score requirements |
| Working professionals | 20 | Workplace speaking + TOEIC for job mobility |

---

## 2. Eligibility Criteria

- Age 18+
- Vietnamese resident (primary language Vietnamese)
- CEFR A2–B2 (self-reported or quick placement)
- Android or iOS smartphone with stable internet
- Willing to complete ≥3 speaking sessions in 14 days
- Consent to voice recording and anonymized feedback use

**Exclude:** Lexora employees, immediate family of team members.

---

## 3. Recruitment Channels

| Channel | Quota | Owner | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Partner universities (HCM, Hanoi) | 20 | PM + Campus lead | Week 13 |
| Facebook groups (TOEIC / IELTS VN) | 15 | Marketing | Week 13 |
| LinkedIn (junior professionals) | 10 | PM | Week 13–14 |
| Existing waitlist (lexora.ai) | 5 | DevOps | Week 13 |

---

## 4. Outreach Copy (Vietnamese)

**Headline:** Tham gia beta Lexora Speaking — luyện nói AI miễn phí 2 tuần

**Body bullets:**

- Luyện nói 5–15 phút với AI coach tiếng Anh
- Phản hồi phát âm và ngữ pháp tức thì
- Miễn phí trong thời gian beta; chỉ cần 3 buổi luyện / 2 tuần
- Ưu tiên 50 người đăng ký sớm nhất

**CTA:** Đăng ký qua Google Form (link in staging invite email)

---

## 5. Screening Form Fields

1. Họ tên, email, SĐT
2. Trường / công ty (optional)
3. Mục tiêu học: TOEIC / Speaking / Business / Other
4. Trình độ tự đánh giá (A1–C1)
5. Thiết bị (Android / iOS / Desktop)
6. Cam kết ≥3 buổi luyện trong 14 ngày (checkbox)
7. Đồng ý ghi âm giọng nói (checkbox — link privacy policy)

---

## 6. Selection & Onboarding

| Step | Action | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Review applications daily | 24h |
| 2 | Select balanced cohort (level, device, goal) | Day 3 |
| 3 | Send invite email + staging link + onboarding guide | Day 4 |
| 4 | Confirm activation (completed onboarding) | Day 7 |
| 5 | Replace drop-offs from waitlist | Ongoing |

**Target activated:** 50 learners with ≥45 completing onboarding by Day 7.

---

## 7. Incentives

- Free Pro access for 30 days post-beta
- Certificate of participation (PDF)
- Entry into raffle: 5 × 3-month Pro subscriptions

---

## 8. Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Applications received | ≥120 |
| Invited | 55 (5 buffer) |
| Activated (onboarding complete) | ≥50 |
| Sessions per learner (14 days) | ≥3 avg |
| Survey response rate | ≥70% |

---

## 9. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Low student response | Partner with 2 university English clubs |
| Device incompatibility | Pre-screen mic/browser in form |
| Drop-off after signup | Reminder email Day 2 + Zalo group support |
