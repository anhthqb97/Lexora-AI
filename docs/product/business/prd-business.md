## Summary

Lexora Business helps Vietnamese professionals practice workplace English through scenario role-plays (meetings, emails, presentations) with formal-tone AI feedback.

---

## Goals

| Goal | Metric |
|---|---|
| Complete first scenario | ≥60% of activated users |
| Weekly return rate | ≥35% |
| Paid conversion | ≥6% |

---

## User Stories

| ID | As a | I want | So that |
|---|---|---|---|
| B-01 | Professional | Practice meeting scenarios | I speak confidently at work |
| B-02 | Professional | Role-play email responses | I write formal English |
| B-03 | Manager | Get formal tone feedback | I avoid casual mistakes |

---

## Scope (MVP)

- 8 business scenarios (meetings, emails, presentations)
- Reuse Speaking session infra with business scenarios
- Formal tone AI tutor prompt
- Progress tracking per scenario

**Out of scope:** Live human coaching, industry-specific certifications
